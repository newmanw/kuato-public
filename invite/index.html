Making the Impossible Possible

Kuato makes issuing recalls easy! Kuato allows military leaders from the Unit Command down to Non-commissioned Officers to recall and provide specific instructions to their team while tracking their response in real time.

Provide text and audio instructions, as well as a location to your team, securely, through the app. When a team member recieves a recall, their location will be reported to the server and they will indicate they are on their way. When they have reported to the designated location, the team leader will check them in.

Team Tracking

Multiple Notification Channels

Teams will be notified via push notification, text message, and email, when a recall is initiated.
